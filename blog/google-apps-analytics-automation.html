Automate Your Ops: Google Apps Script and Google Analytics

Gab · April 2026 · 6 min read

Data is useless if you don't look at it. But logging into Google Analytics every day is a chore. I prefer my data to come to me.

This is where Google Apps Script shines.

The Automated Dashboard

I wrote a simple script (well, Claude wrote it for me) that connects to the Google Analytics 4 API. Every Monday morning at 8:00 AM, it does the following:

  1. Fetches the pageviews, user counts, and conversion rates for the last week.
  2. Pastes this data into a specific row in a Google Sheet.
  3. Calculates the week-over-week growth.
  4. Sends me an email summary via Gmail with the key highlights.

I don't open Analytics anymore. I just check my email. If something is red, I investigate. If it's green, I keep going.

Google Apps Script is the glue of the internet. It's free, it runs in the cloud, and it connects everything in the Google ecosystem.
