Monetizing Your Micro-SaaS with Stripe and Kit

Gab · April 2026 · 6 min read

Building a product is easy. Selling it is hard. Usually, setting up payments involves integrating an API, handling webhooks, and managing user databases. That's a lot of code just to take $10 from someone.

There is a better way: Stripe Payment Links + Kit.

The No-Code Commerce Stack

Here is how I sell my digital products and courses without writing a single line of backend code:

  1. Create a Product in Stripe. Set the price, upload an image.
  2. Generate a Payment Link. Stripe gives you a simple URL. When someone clicks it, they go to a hosted checkout page. It handles taxes, receipts, and mobile wallets (Apple Pay/Google Pay).
  3. Connect to Kit (formerly ConvertKit). Using a simple automation (Zapier or direct integration), when a purchase happens in Stripe, the customer is added to a sequence in Kit.
  4. Deliver the Product via Email. Kit sends them the welcome email with the download link or access code.

That's it. No shopping cart software. No Shopify monthly fees. Just Stripe taking a small percentage and Kit handling the delivery.

This setup scales from your first dollar to your first million. Keep it simple.
