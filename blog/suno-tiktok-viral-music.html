Gab · April 2026 · 5 min read

TikTok is an audio-first platform. If you want to go viral, you don't just need good video; you need good sound. But hiring a producer to make a custom track is expensive and slow.

That's where Suno comes in.

Creating Original Audio in Minutes

Suno generates full songs—vocals and instrumentals—from a simple text prompt. Need a "lo-fi hip hop beat about productivity"? Or a "hyperpop banger about AI"? Suno creates it in 30 seconds.

Why does this matter for TikTok?

Because original audio is the key to trends. When you upload a video with a catchy, original Suno track, you own that sound. If other people use it, they are effectively advertising your channel for free.

The Strategy

  1. Identify a niche (e.g., "coding humor").
  2. Generate a short, catchy song with Suno ("Python is better than Java rap").
  3. Post a simple video using the audio.
  4. Encourage others to use the sound.
