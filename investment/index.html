Valuation Metrics

P/E Ratio

How many dollars investors pay per dollar of earnings. Trailing vs forward, sector benchmarks, and earnings quality.

PEG Ratio

P/E normalized by earnings growth. Peter Lynch's framework for determining if a growth premium is justified.

Free Cash Flow Yield

Cash generation relative to market cap. The metric that catches what earnings-based ratios miss.
